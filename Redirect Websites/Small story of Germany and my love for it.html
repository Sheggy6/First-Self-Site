My home country and my new found love:

Germany!

For a long time I was not a fan, believing the distaste of my Father and adopting it as my own. That being said, after having been back for almost ten years now, I am happy to say, I have found a new found appreciation of my home land. The geography, the people, the almost quaint fairy tale like status of small towns, and its rich, deep, history. Everything about this country has moved me to want to stay here, or at the very least, continue to stay involved in this nation.
